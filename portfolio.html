About Me

"When you want to succeed as bad as you want to breathe, then you will be successful."
      -Eric Thomas

One of my favorite quotes and one that I try to live by every day. I'm 22 years old and currently a senior at Purdue University, majoring in Computer Graphics Technology with a focus in 3D animation. I've been modeling for quite a few years and have always had a love and curiosity for animation. Making a model, or scene, is one thing, but the love truly begins with the chance to bring it to life!

I have a split love and hatred for my field of study. Being a Computer Graphics major I get the chance to create short animations and tell stories to my friends and family in truly creative ways. On the other hand, I now understand how most effects are created in Motion Pictures, and the times I don't, I spend most of the movie trying to figure out what they could have done to create it! In both cases, I do love and enjoy my time. Whether the time is spent creating or learning, the art of motion is truly special, and my love for bringing still objects to life, will remain.
